The problem

Most incident reporting is fragmented—shared in calls, WhatsApp groups, and informal messages. Details get missed, reports get lost, response is delayed, and residents have no clear visibility on progress.

The solution

HawkEye centralises reporting into one structured workflow where incidents are logged with the right details, routed to the right teams, tracked from submission to resolution, and updated with clarity.
